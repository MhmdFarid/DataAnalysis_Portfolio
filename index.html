Welcome to my Data Analysis Portfolio!

I am a data analyst with a background in mechanical engineering. I am highly motivated and dedicated to pursuing a career in data analytics. Demonstrated my keen interest in the field by actively seeking knowledge through online courses and applying acquired skills to real-world projects. I gained experience in using various tools and technologies to collect, clean, and analyze data to inform business decisions.
